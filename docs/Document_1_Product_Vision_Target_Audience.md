Document 1: Product Vision & Target Audience
Project: Nivå
Persona: "Senior Analytiker" (Senior Analyst)
1. Vision Nivå is a strategic decision-support app for home buyers that replaces uncertainty and "gut feeling" with source-verified data. The app acts as a personal financial analyst, helping users understand the true cost of a property purchase by linking the housing association's (BRF) finances with the user's private financial profile.
2. Target Audience
* Active home buyers in Swedish metropolitan areas.
* Couples buying together ("Hushållsläge" / Household Mode).
* Users seeking security and a deep understanding of interest rate sensitivity and hidden risks in a BRF.
3. Tone and Style The tone is objective, professional, and data-driven. The interface should feel like a precision tool (similar to modern fintech), focusing on clarity and transparency.
